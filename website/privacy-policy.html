Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how AgriGrow collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use the app and related services.

Last updated: April 9, 2026 Jurisdiction: Assam, India App: AgriGrow

Who We Are

AgriGrow is a free farming app developed and operated by a small team led by Prithviman Bhattacharjee, based in Silchar, Assam, India.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information when you use AgriGrow:

  • Onboarding details such as selected language, whether you identify as a gardener or professional farmer, and the crops you are interested in.
  • Technical data such as your IP address, approximate device and browser or app information, logs, cookies, analytics identifiers, and usage activity.
  • Account details if you sign in with Google or create an account using your name and phone number.
  • Images and related inputs that you upload for crop disease detection and plant health analysis.
  • Notification and engagement data if you allow push notifications or interact with ads, analytics, or in-app campaigns.

How We Use Your Information

We use information to operate and improve AgriGrow, including to:

  • personalize onboarding and show tools, crops, and workflows relevant to your interests;
  • support features such as seed calculators, irrigation tools, fertilizer calculators, crop planning, crop tracking, and other farm-support services;
  • analyze uploaded crop images and return disease detection results through Gemini;
  • maintain security, prevent misuse, troubleshoot issues, and understand how the app is being used;
  • send notifications, updates, and service-related messages where allowed; and
  • measure performance, ads, and product usage through analytics and related technologies.

Google Sign-In and Gemini

You may choose to continue without creating an account, sign in with Google credentials, or create an account manually with your name and phone number.

When you upload a crop image for disease detection, the image may be processed using Gemini to generate diagnostic insights and recommendations. AI-based outputs are assistive only and should not be treated as a guaranteed agricultural, scientific, or professional opinion.

Cookies, Analytics, Ads, and Notifications

AgriGrow may use cookies, similar technologies, analytics tools, advertising services, and push notifications to understand usage, improve performance, communicate updates, and support product growth. Depending on the device or platform, you may be able to manage some of these permissions or preferences in your browser, device settings, or account settings.

How We Share Information

We may share information in limited situations such as:

  • with service providers or infrastructure partners that help us operate the app and related features;
  • with Google services where you choose Google sign-in or where Gemini is used for disease detection workflows;
  • when required by law, regulation, legal process, or a valid government request; and
  • to protect the rights, safety, security, or integrity of AgriGrow, our users, or the public.

Data Retention

We keep information for as long as reasonably necessary to operate the app, maintain records, improve services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our terms. Retention periods may vary depending on the type of data and how the feature is used.

Your Choices

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your account or personal data by contacting us. We may need to verify your identity before completing a request.

If you do not want to create an account, you can continue using parts of AgriGrow without logging in, although some features may be limited.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information, but no method of storage, transmission, or internet-based processing is completely secure. You use the app and upload content at your own discretion.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised date.
